About YDSA

The future is ours to win

Democratic Socialists of America (DSA) is the largest socialist organization in America, and YDSA is the Youth & campus wing of that org. DSA has over 94,000 members, including congresswomen Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez & Rashida Tlaib. As socialists, we believe that:

  • The economy should be run democratically by workers
  • The liberation of all oppressed peoples requires the abolition of Capitalism
  • The US empire must be dismantled globally
  • We can win these victories through working class struggle!

Our Organizing

Purdue YDSA organizes on a number of different fronts to build student and worker power at our university, including anti-racist organizing, electoral campaigns, and direct action. See some highlights below.

Organizing

Pave the way to socialism

From standing against ICE to supporting housing for all people, we have turned out to defend our communities and present a strong left-wing voice at public actions.

Political Education

Learn about socialism and other working-class issues

Curious what socialists believe? Interested in learning more about socialist theory, history, and praxis? These are the meetings for you! Every other meeting our chapter hosts is dedicated to political education. We often present about topics related to our organizing works and current events.

Reading Group

Discuss socialist theory and praxis

We've held Debate Watch Parties, Political Education Sessions, Reading Groups, and volunteer events in an effort to foster a leftist social community at Purdue.

Social Events

Build comradery and have fun

We don't just organize! We host events such as t-shirt printing, presentation nights, movie nights, collage making, and potlucks. Attend a social event to get to know members of the chapter better and decompress a little.

Executive Committee

Purdue YDSA is lead by a democratically elected Executive Committee, which establishes chapter priorities, organizes meetings, manages funds, and ensures that the chapter grows and develops in healthy ways.
